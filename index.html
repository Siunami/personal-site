歡迎光臨, welcome!

I do interface research exploring ways to expand what we can perceive and understand.

My current focus is on prototyping a text editor that can help people synthesize messy, unstructured materials. More to share soon! I’ve done research at NYU focused on creativity support tools, developed interfaces at Midjourney and worked as an interaction designer alongside Hugh Dubberly.

Friends have described me as an old soul. Patient, perceptive, persistent. Please have a look around and feel free to say hi.
